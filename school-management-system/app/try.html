# Create Next App

1. Click on Fees from the side panel

2. Click on Receive Payment for 5

3. Click on Fees Received / Fees Being Paid (GHC) *

4. Fill out the rest of the required fields to complete the payment.

5. Click on Pay Fee

6. Choose whether or not you want to print a receipt from the prompt that follows it.
